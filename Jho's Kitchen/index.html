About Us

Welcome to Jho's Kitchen, where every bite feels like a taste of home. We serve authentic, homemade Nigerian dishes that remind you of those cherished family gatherings, filled with love and comfort. From rich Jollof rice and spicy suya, to savory moin moin and tender pounded yam with egusi, our meals bring the heart of Africa to your table.
Feeling thirsty? Our freshly made juices like zobo and chapman, along with our signature cocktails, will refresh and delight. Whether it’s a chilled Palm Wine, a zesty ginger drink, or an exotic fruit cocktail, we’ve got something to quench every thirst.
Looking for something sweet? Indulge in our creamy yoghurts, delicious chin chin, and our homemade ice creams. If you're planning a special occasion or just want to unwind, our cozy reservation spaces offer the perfect homely atmosphere to enjoy your meal. And for those busy days, our fast delivery ensures your favorite Nigerian dishes—hot and fresh—right at your doorstep.
At Jho's Kitchen, it’s not just about the food, it’s about bringing the warmth of home to you with every meal, every drink, and every moment.
